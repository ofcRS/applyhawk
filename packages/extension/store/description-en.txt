ApplyHawk — AI-powered job application tool that personalizes your resume and generates cover letters for every job you apply to.

HOW IT WORKS

1. Set up your base resume once (upload PDF or fill manually)
2. Open any job posting on supported platforms
3. Click the ApplyHawk button — AI analyzes the job, scores your fit, and generates a tailored resume + cover letter
4. Download your personalized PDF or apply directly (HH.ru)

KEY FEATURES

• Smart Resume Personalization — AI rewrites your experience to match each job's requirements while preserving your authentic voice
• Fit Assessment — Instantly see how well you match a position with strengths and gaps analysis
• Cover Letter Generation — Professional cover letters for each application, highlighting your most relevant experience
• PDF Export — Download personalized resumes as professionally formatted PDFs with Cyrillic support
• ATS Form Fill — AI-powered auto-fill for application forms on ATS platforms
• Multi-Language — Full English and Russian support, auto-detects job description language

SUPPORTED PLATFORMS

• HH.ru — Full integration with direct API, auto-apply
• LinkedIn — Job page detection + one-click generation
• Indeed — Job page detection + one-click generation
• Glassdoor — Job page detection + one-click generation
• Greenhouse — ATS job page detection
• Lever — ATS job page detection
• Workday — ATS job page detection
• And more via universal detection

PRIVACY FIRST

All your data stays in your browser. ApplyHawk does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Your resume and API key never leave your device. AI processing uses your own OpenRouter API key — we never see your data.
